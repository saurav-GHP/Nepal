Nepal

Capital: Kathmandu

Population: 30+ million

Official language: Nepali

Highest mountain: Mount Everest

Region: South Asia

Overview

Nepal is a mountainous country in South Asia located between India and China. It is famous for its stunning Himalayan landscapes and cultural heritage.

The country contains eight of the ten highest mountains in the world, including Mount Everest.

Geography

Nepal’s geography ranges from the Terai plains in the south to the Himalayan mountains in the north. Rivers, forests, and valleys form diverse ecosystems.

Natural Beauty

Nepal is one of the most beautiful countries in the world. Snow-covered mountains, green forests, lakes, and rivers create breathtaking scenery.

Culture

Nepal’s culture is influenced by Hindu and Buddhist traditions. Festivals like Dashain and Tihar are celebrated across the country.

Tourism

Tourism is one of Nepal’s most important industries. Trekking, mountaineering, and cultural tourism attract millions of visitors every year.
